I am interested in large language models, test-time scaling, and inference-time algorithms that make model behavior more capable, diverse, and efficient.

My recent work studies **Exploratory Sampling (ESamp)**, a decoding-time method that encourages LLMs to explore semantically diverse reasoning paths through online latent distillation.
